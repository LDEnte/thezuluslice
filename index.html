About the Tour

The Zulu Slice is an annual invitational golf tour played at one of South Africa's finest courses — Champagne Sports Resort nestled in the dramatic Drakensberg mountains. Each year 12 members compete across rounds for the coveted Zulu Slice title, with handicaps tracked throughout the season.

Full details for the 2027 edition — including dates, format, prize structure, and registration — will be posted here as they are confirmed. Check back soon.
